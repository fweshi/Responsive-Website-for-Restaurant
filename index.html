About Us

Welcome to FingerLicking Restaurant. Indulge your senses in a culinary journey like no other at FingerLicking Restaurant. Nestled in the heart of Lagos State, our restaurant offers an unforgettable dining experience that tantalizes the taste buds and captivates the soul.

Cuisine

At FingerLicking Restaurant, we celebrate the artistry of cuisine, blending traditional flavors with modern techniques to create dishes that are both innovative and comforting. Our menu showcases a diverse selection of Spanish Cuisine delicacies, expertly crafted by our talented chefs using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients.

Atmosphere

Step into a world of elegance and sophistication as you enter FingerLicking Restaurant. Our inviting ambiance strikes the perfect balance between chic and cozy, providing the ideal setting for intimate dinners, lively gatherings, and everything in between. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a memorable meal, our restaurant sets the stage for an unforgettable dining experience.

Location

Conveniently located in Lagos State, FingerLicking Restaurant is easily accessible from The Lekki-Ikoyi Link Bridge. Whether you're a local resident or visiting from out of town, our prime location makes us the perfect destination for food enthusiasts seeking exceptional cuisine in a vibrant setting.
